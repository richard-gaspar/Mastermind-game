MasterMind game rules

The computer has selected a secret combination of 4 colored circles and you have to guess that combination in 10 or fewer tries to win. Be aware: each color can be multiple times in the combination!

How to play . . .

To create your guess click each apparent colored circle until you've filled the row with your combination (you can delete your guess by clicking "delete" button). Then click the "check my guess" button. Each time you submit a guess the machine will use score circles to let you know how close that guess is. For each guess a black score circle indicates that one of your circle is the right color in the right position. A white score circle indicates that one of your circles is the right color but in the wrong position. Use the score to guide your next guess. If your guess scores 4 black score cirlces within 10 tries, you win. To begin a new game click the "New game" button. Good luck!
